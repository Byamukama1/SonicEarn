About SonicEarn

Pioneering innovative investment solutions since 1945

Founded in Nagano, Japan in 1945, SonicEarn began as a financial instrument manufacturer before pioneering the first electronic trading terminal in 1968. Our name "SO-NIC" (son of SC) reflects our legacy of innovation.

Today, we've helped over 500,000 investors globally grow their wealth through our unique investment models. Our fixed-yield shares program has paid out over UGX 250 billion in earnings since its inception.

Through our "Environmental Vision 2050", we're committed to sustainable investing practices that balance performance with reduced ecological impact.
